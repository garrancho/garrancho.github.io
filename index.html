About Bruno

Bruno leads the BBC's information security engineering and architecture development. With over 30 years of passion for technology, he brings a wealth of hands-on experience to his role.

Bruno is a lifelong learner who believes in the power of experiential education to spur holistic personal growth. His diverse background spans startups and large corporations alike, yet he finds common threads that continue to intrigue him.

When not working or spending time with family, you may find Bruno tinkering with the latest gadget or pondering how to make systems more intuitive.
